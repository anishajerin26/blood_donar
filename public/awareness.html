Why Donate Blood?

Every blood donation can save up to 3 lives. Donating blood is safe, simple, and takes only 15 minutes.

Quick Blood Facts

  • Blood cannot be manufactured — it can only come from donors.
  • Every 2 seconds, someone in the world needs blood.
  • One donor can help trauma patients, cancer patients, and children in need.
  • Donating blood improves your own cardiovascular health.
